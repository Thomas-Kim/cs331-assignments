  {Q1}
  Prove each of the following heuristics to be ideal or not ideal for scheduling jobs on a machine while minimizing:
  \begin{align*}
      \sum_{i = 1}^{n} w_iC_i
  \end{align*}
  1. Smallest time first. \\
  2. Most important first. \\
  3. Maximum $\frac{w(i)}{t(i)}$. \\
\end{problem}
